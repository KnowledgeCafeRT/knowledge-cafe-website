who are we?

coffee

Big thanks to Hochland Kaffee for keeping us buzzing! For over 60 years, they've been perfecting ethical direct trade and roasting with care, so every cup is full of flavor and good vibes.

Students

We're ESB Business School students running a non-profit café for students on campus. Tuesdays IMX students serve and thurdays IB students take over

Mission

We provide high quality and affordable specialty coffee to the university community and bring people closer through highly engaged baristas as well as high value and sustainable materials to promote the social exchange between study programmes and faculties.
