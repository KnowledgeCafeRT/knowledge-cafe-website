Coffee

Big thanks to Hochland Kaffee for keeping us buzzing! For over 60 years, they've been perfecting ethical direct trade and roasting with care, so every cup is full of flavor and good vibes.

Who are we?

We’re ESB Business School students running a non-profit café for the campus community. Tuesdays IMX students serve and Thursdays IB students take over.

Mission

We provide high quality and affordable specialty coffee to the university community and bring people closer through engaged baristas and sustainable materials that promote exchange between study programmes and faculties.

Students

Knowledge Café is run solely for charitable purposes. We focus on high-quality coffee, meaningful connections, and a cozy space for everyone to recharge.
